Isabella Script
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
Isabella Script is handwritten stylish copperplate calligraphy fonts, combines from copperplate to contemporary typeface with a dancing baseline, classic and elegant touch.
Isabella features 300+ glyphs and 118 alternate characters. including initial and terminal letters, alternates, ligatures and multiple language support.

Files included: 
- Isabella.otf 
- Isabella.ttf
- Web Font (eot, svg, woff)

Can be used for various purposes.such as headings, signature, logos, wedding invitation, t-shirt, letterhead, signage, lable, news, posters, badges etc. 
To enable the OpenType Stylistic alternates, you need a program that supports OpenType features such as Adobe Illustrator CS, Adobe Indesign & CorelDraw X6-X7.
